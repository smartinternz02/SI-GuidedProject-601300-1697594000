Potato Classification using CNN

Potato Classification:


Potato leaf classification, employing Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), represents a cutting-edge approach that harnesses the capabilities of deep learning to categorize and distinguish various potato leaf types based on their visual attributes. CNNs, a specialized class of artificial neural networks, are adept at processing and identifying image patterns. The workflow commences with a substantial dataset of potato leaf images, meticulously annotated with their respective categories. The CNN model then embarks on the journey of extracting crucial features and patterns from these images through a series of convolutional, pooling, and activation layers. Convolutional layers employ small filters to systematically scan the input leaf images, capturing diverse characteristics like vein patterns, textures, and leaf shapes.
